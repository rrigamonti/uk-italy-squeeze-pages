**File:** `squeeze-page-uk.html` — Production-ready HTML/CSS squeeze page

## Overview

A refined, premium-designed squeeze page following the frontend-design skill guidelines:
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (headers) + Source Sans 3 (body) — distinctive, editorial quality
- **Colour:** Deep teal (#1E5C57) primary, amber gold accents, cream background — sophisticated, premium feel
- **Design Elements:** Glass-morphism cards, subtle shadows, clean hierarchy, premium spacing
- **No generic patterns:** No purple gradients, no Inter/Roboto fonts, no Bootstrap-style components

---

## Page Structure

1. **Navigation bar** — Logo + trust signals (GDPR, 1,200+ directors)
2. **Hero section** — Bold headline with italic accent, subhead, email capture form
3. **Visual preview card** — Floating blueprint preview showing what's inside
4. **Social proof stats** — 3-column stats (23 companies, 34% growth, 94% implementation)
5. **Content grid** — 6 feature cards showing blueprint contents
6. **Final CTA** — Full-width dark teal section with email form
7. **Footer** — Legal + compliance info

## Technical Details

- **Framework:** Tailwind CSS via CDN
- **Icons:** Lucide (clean, modern)
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Source Sans 3)
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first, single column on mobile, 2-column on desktop
- **Form:** Ready for email service integration
