## Usage Notes

- Ensure text‑overlay areas have sufficient contrast/empty space for copy
- Colour palette: blues (#0F4C81, #1E6FB9), white, accent gold (#FFC72C)
- Save as high‑quality PNG/JPG with appropriate compression for web
