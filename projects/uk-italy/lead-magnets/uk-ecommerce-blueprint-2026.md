#### Strategy 1: Mobile-First Checkout

If your mobile conversion rate is under 2.5%, you have a leak.

**Quick Wins:**
- Reduce checkout steps to 3 or fewer
- Enable Apple Pay / Google Pay (removes friction)

**UK-Specific Subscription Best Practices:**
- Offer quarterly, not just monthly (matches UK budgeting habits)
- Include "pause" option (reduces cancellations by 34%)
- Pre-notify 3 days before charge (UK consumer expectation)

## 3. Post-Brexit Compliance Mastery

### The Current Landscape (March 2026)

Three years post-Brexit, the compliance landscape has stabilised — but complexity remains.

**Key Changes Effective 2026:**
- New UKCA marking requirements (CE no longer accepted in UK)
- EU Import Control System 2 (ICS2) fully enforced
- UK Border Target Operating Model phases complete
- New simplified customs procedures for SMEs

### The Compliance Checklist

#### Pre-Shipment

- [ ] **HS Code Verification:** Check your commodity codes on [trade-tariff.service.gov.uk](https://www.trade-tariff.service.gov.uk)
- [ ] **Rules of Origin:** Obtain proof that goods qualify for zero tariff (if applicable)
- [ ] **Commercial Invoice:** Include EORI numbers, accurate values, and clear descriptions
- [ ] **Packing List:** Match to invoice exactly

#### Customs Declaration

- [ ] **CHIEF vs CDS:** Ensure you're using Customs Declaration Service (CDS) — CHIEF is being phased out
- [ ] **Deferment Account:** Set up if shipping regularly (cash flow benefit)
- [ ] **Postponed VAT Accounting:** Essential for cash flow management

#### EU Shipments

- [ ] **ICS2 Submission:** Submit safety and security data before shipment
- [ ] **EU EORI:** Required if storing goods in EU
- [ ] **OSS Registration:** If selling B2C to EU consumers (threshold €10,000)

### Common Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

**Mistake 1: Wrong HS Codes**
- Impact: Unexpected duties, delays, potential seizures
- Fix: Use HMRC's Tariff Classification Service for uncertainty

**Mistake 2: Incorrect Origin Declaration**
- Impact: Full duty rates applied
- Fix: Obtain supplier declarations proving origin

**Mistake 3: Missing Safety Documentation**
- Impact: Goods held at border
- Fix: Pre-submit via ICS2 for all EU shipments

## 4. Tax Efficiency Strategies

### R&D Tax Credits for E-commerce

Most e-commerce businesses under-claim. If you've developed:
- Custom logistics software
- Personalisation algorithms
- Inventory management systems
- Multi-channel integrations

...you likely qualify for **R&D tax relief**.

**2026 Rates:**
- SME scheme: Up to 33% of qualifying costs
- RDEC scheme: 20% of qualifying costs

**Typical Claim Size:** £15k–£150k depending on development spend

### Patent Box

If you've patented unique technology (product or process), profits attributable to that patent are taxed at **10%** instead of 25%.

### Creative Industry Tax Relief

E-commerce businesses in fashion, design, or media may qualify for:
- Fashion Industry Tax Relief
- Design Tax Relief

### VAT Efficiency

**Flat Rate Scheme:** Consider if turnover is under £150k
- Simplified VAT calculation
- Potential savings if low input VAT

**Annual Accounting:** If regular VAT refunds
- Submit one return per year
- Improve cash flow with monthly payments

### SEIS/EIS for Investment

Raising capital? Structure for SEIS/EIS eligibility:
- Investors get 50% (SEIS) or 30% (EIS) income tax relief
- Capital gains exemptions
- Loss relief if company fails

**2026 Limits:**
- SEIS: £250k max raise
- EIS: £5m max raise per year

## 5. Platform Recommendations by Sector

### Fashion & Apparel

**Best Fit: Shopify Plus**
- Why: Handles high SKU counts, integrates with 3PLs, strong internationalisation
- Alternative: BigCommerce (better for B2B fashion)

**Key Integrations:**
- Klarna/Clearpay (BNPL essential)
- Returnly or Happy Returns (high return category)
- Vue.ai (personalisation)

### Electronics & Gadgets

**Best Fit: Magento (Adobe Commerce)**
- Why: Complex product configurations, warranty management, technical specs
- Alternative: Shopify Plus + custom app development

**Key Integrations:**
- Gorgias (technical support)
- Extend (warranty upsells)
- FedEx/DHL (tracked shipping essential)

### Home & Garden

**Best Fit: BigCommerce**
- Why: Built-in B2B features, strong product options, good for bulky items
- Alternative: WooCommerce (if heavy content marketing)

**Key Integrations:**
- ShipperHQ (complex shipping rules for bulky items)
- Yotpo (reviews crucial for this category)

### Food & Beverage

**Best Fit: Shopify + Recharge**
- Why: Subscription models dominate, freshness dating
- Alternative: Craft Commerce (if heavily custom)

**Key Integrations:**
- Recharge (subscriptions)
- Freshly (inventory by expiry date)
- Loop (subscription management)

### Health & Beauty

**Best Fit: Shopify Plus**
- Why: Fast-growing sector, influencer marketing integration, global expansion
- Alternative: Centra (if wholesale + D2C)

**Key Integrations:**
- Okendo (reviews with photos)
- Klaviyo (segmentation essential)
- Gatsby (influencer identification)
