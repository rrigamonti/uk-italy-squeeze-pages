### Critical Success Factors
#### Must‑Haves (Non‑negotiable)
1. **Tracking** = GA4 + Meta Pixel + UTM parameters
2. **Email automation** = Immediate welcome + 7‑day sequence
3. **Mobile optimization** = 40%+ of traffic is mobile
4. **Legal compliance** = GDPR + privacy policy

#### Nice‑to‑Haves (Week 2+)
1. **Chat support** = Live chat/WhatsApp for questions
2. **Social proof** = Testimonials/trust badges
3. **Exit‑intent popup** = Capture abandoning visitors
4. **SMS follow‑up** = For high‑value leads

#### Red Flags (Investigate immediately)
1. Conversion rate < 10% after 500 visits
2. Email open rate < 30% (sequence day 1)
3. Page load time > 4 seconds
4. Form submission errors > 2%
